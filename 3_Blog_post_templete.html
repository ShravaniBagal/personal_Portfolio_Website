The Journey into Web Development

By Shravani Bagal | Published on August 1, 2025

Web development has become one of the most exciting fields in today's technology landscape. Whether you're creating simple websites or complex applications, the journey is filled with learning and creativity.

Why I Started

It all began with curiosity. I always wondered how websites were built. After exploring a few tutorials and experimenting with HTML and CSS, I realized how much I enjoy bringing things to life on a screen.

What I’ve Learned So Far

  • HTML5 for structuring content
  • CSS for styling and layout
  • JavaScript for interactivity

Advice for Beginners

If you’re just starting, don’t be afraid of making mistakes. Build small projects, break things, fix them — that’s the best way to learn.

Keep practicing, and over time, you'll be amazed by what you can create.
